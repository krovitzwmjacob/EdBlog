Symbolism and Mental Health Lesson Plan

August 2, 2023

This blog post features and original lesson plan that concerns the topic of symbolism (an object, shape, sign, or character used to represent something else) and combining it with mental health. This lesson takes the idea of an object having significant meaning and representing something else and connecting it to student's own lives. Students, with their understanding of symbolism, will create a symbol of their own and descibe its meaning.

All materials are available FREE within the post. Enjoy!

How Teachers can Strengthen Student Mental Health and Wellbeing

July 16, 2023

In this blog post, I discuss ways teachers instill mental health strategies and strengthen our students' mental wellbeing just by being teachers. Using research from Raymond Wallar's book, Fostering Child and Adolescent Mental Health in the Classroom, and my own observations and anecdotal evidence, I share ways that teachers can (and most likely already do) to promote positive mental health practices within our students.
