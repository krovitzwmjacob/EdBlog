Common Sense Education

Common Sense Education provides research, lesson plans, and resources surrounding digital citzenship. They have a wealth of knowledge and resources for teachers, educators, adminstrators, counselors, district staff, and parents on different aspects of digital accountability and online safety. For educators, there is an online course that provides accreditation once completed.
