Purpose

The purpose of my blog is to share information and resources about mental health and educaiton. Mental health is an extremely crucial aspect to take care of in everyone's lives, especially our growing students.

With this website, I aim to share ways to incorporate and nurture mental health in the classroom, for your students and you. I plan on discussing topics such as promoting positive mental health in the classroom, ways to take care of your mental health as a teacher, strategies and tools for mental health in the classroom, content-related mental health activities, and more!

Latest Blog Post

How Teachers can Strengthen Student Mental Health and Wellbeing

In this blog post, I discuss ways teachers instill mental health strategies and strengthen our students' mental wellbeing just by being teachers. Using research from Raymond Wallar's book, Fostering Child and Adolescent Mental Health in the Classroom, and my own observations and anecdotal evidence, I share ways that teachers can (and most likely already do) to promote positive mental health practices within our students.

All blog posts have an audio version within each post.
