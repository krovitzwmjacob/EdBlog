Purpose

The purpose of my blog is to share information and resources about mental health and educaiton. Mental health is an extremely crucial aspect to take care of in everyone's lives, especially our growing students. Our students carry a lot in their minds each day to and from school. With this website, I aim to share ways to incorporate and nurture mental health in the classroom, for your students and you.
